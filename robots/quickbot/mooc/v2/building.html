Rocker Switch Wiring Assembly

Number to build: 1

Parts

  1. 1 - 2-pin Female Smaller JST Uh-oh Connector
  2. 1 - 3-pin Rocker Switch
  3. 2 - 2-pin Male Red JST Battery Connector
  4. 2 - 1/2” Heat Shrink Tubing

Tools

  1. Wire Strippers
  2. Wire Cutters
  3. Soldering Iron

Instructions

  1. (2) Cut red JST battery connector wires to 3.5” and strip 1/4” from ends.
  2. (2) Untwist 1 JST wire 1” and strip 1/4” from end.

  3. (2) Cut 1/2” heat shrink tubing


  4. (2) Twist all 3 black wires together, insert through heat shrink tube.

  5. (2) Twist all 3 red wires together, insert through heat shrink tube.


  6. Solder black wires to middle pin of switch.
  7. Solder red wires to pin on “O” side of switch.
    • Pro tip: Solder wires together first, then solder to lead of the switch.

  8. Heat shrink the tubing around solder points.

Encoder Cable Assembly

Number to build: 2

Parts

  1. 1 - Encoder Ribbon Cable
  2. 5 - Female Molex Crimp Pins
  3. 1 - 5-pin Molex Header
  4. 2 - 1x2-pin 0.1” Crimp Connector Housing
  5. 1 - 1x1-pin 0.1” Crimp Connector Housing
  6. 5 - 0.1” Male Crimp Pins

Tools

  1. Wire Strippers
  2. Wire Cutters
  3. Crimpers
  4. Needle Nose Pliers

Instructions

  1. Separate 3/4” of the ribbon cable in to individual wires.
  2. Strip 1/8” of insulation from each write and twist ends they are no frayed.
  3. Crimp female Molex crimp pin to each wire.
    • Note: Make sure to crimp pin in correct orientation.**


  4. Separate 3/4” of the other end of the ribbon cable into individual wires.
  5. Strip 1/8” of insulation from each wire and twist ends so they are not frayed.
  6. Crimp 0.1” male crimp pin to each wire.
  7. Insert white & gray or yellow & orange wires into 1x2 0.1” crimp connector housing.
  8. Insert black & blue or green & brown wire into 1x2 0.1” crimp connector housing.

  9. Insert purple or red into 1x1 0.1” crimp connector housing.
